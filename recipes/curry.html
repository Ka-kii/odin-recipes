Curry

Description

This recipe is the best thing you will ever make and taste.

Ingredients

  • tofu
  • panko
  • flour
  • eggs
  • rice
  • curry-blocks
  • onions
  • potatoes
  • carrots

Steps

  1. peel and cut the carrots, an onion and potatoes into bitesized pieces and fry them in a pot with some vegetable broth seasoning.
  2. While frying cut your tofu into small slices. Then, add water to your vegetables, enough to cover them. Now you put a few curry-blocks and let it simmer. Put the blocks one by one and add to taste and consistency.
  3. While this is cooking you can coat your tofu with flour, egg and panko and fry it. Serve while still hot.
