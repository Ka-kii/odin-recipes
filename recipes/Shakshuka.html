Shakshuka with Feta

Description

SShakshuka with feta: With this quick recipe you can easily prepare the spicy, healthy and vegetarian breakfast yourself. Bon appetit!

Ingredients

  • red pepper
  • Red onion
  • Garlic cloves
  • olive oil
  • Salt
  • pepper
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Paprika powder (sweet)
  • Cumin (cumin)
  • Agave syrup
  • Tomato paste
  • whole & peeled tomatoes (can)
  • Water
  • Eggs (size M)
  • Feta
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Wash the pepper, quarter it, remove the seeds and cut into fine strips. Peel the onion and garlic cloves and chop finely. Heat oil in a pan and fry peppers and onions over medium heat for 4-5 minutes until onions are translucent.
  2. Add garlic and spices and fry for 2 minutes . Add agave syrup and tomato paste and mix everything well.
  3. Add the canned tomatoes to the vegetables and press them down carefully with a spatula. Add water and mix well. Simmer gently for 10-15 minutes until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  4. Use a spoon to make four small wells in the sauce and carefully break in the eggs. Season eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper. Place the lid on the pan and cook the eggs, covered, over low heat for 6-8 minutes , until the whites are set and the yolks are still relatively runny.
  5. In the meantime, crumble the feta cheese . Pick off the parsley leaves and chop roughly. Before serving, top the shakshuka with feta and garnish with parsley leaves.
